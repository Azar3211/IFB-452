How SeaTrace Works

1

Capture

Fishermen record catch data including location, species, and fishing method directly on the blockchain.

2

Process

Processing facilities verify incoming shipments and add processing information to the blockchain record.

3

Distribute

Distributors confirm chain of custody and add transportation and storage conditions to the ledger.

4

Verify

Retailers and consumers can scan QR codes to access the complete history of their seafood products.
